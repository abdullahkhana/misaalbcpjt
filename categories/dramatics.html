ART PERFORMANCES

This category features stage performances like monologues, plays, stand-up comedy, and dance. It also includes themed fashion walks, such as Eastern Wear and 90’s Classics. Judged on creativity, expression, and audience engagement, art performances bring energy and storytelling to the spotlight.

ART PERFORMANCES

MONOLOGUE

  • Duration: Not more than 5 minutes

  • Participants will be required to either recreate a monologue from a film/TV series or act out an original monologue.

  • Participants must send their scripts prior to their performances.

  • Awards:

    • Best Monologue

    • Runners-up

  • Rules:

    • No profanity, vulgarity, or blasphemous content, as it will result in disqualification.

THEATRE

  • Duration: Teams must perform a short dramatic piece within a 20-minute limit.

  • Teams can choose their own theme.

  • Awards:

    • Best Play

    • Runners-up

    • Best Script

    • Best Production

    • Best Actor

    • Best Actress

    • Best Supporting Actor

    • Best Supporting Actress

  • Rules:

    • No profanity, vulgarity, or blasphemous content, as it will result in disqualification.

STAND-UP COMEDY

  • Duration: 5-10 minutes per participant.

  • Participants can choose their own content/topic.

  • Awards:

    • Winner

    • Runners-up

  • Rules:

    • Content must be audience-appropriate and should not promote hate, discrimination, or violence.

DANCE

  • Duration: Not more than 10 minutes.

  • Teams should consist of a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 7 members.

  • Participants can choose their own genre.

  • Awards:

    • Winner

    • Runners-up

  • Rules:

    • No vulgar content, as it will result in disqualification.
