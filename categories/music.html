MUSIC

Dive into the rhythm and melody with our diverse music categories! Whether you're a solo singer, an instrumentalist, a duo performer, part of a band, or a hip-hop artist, there's a platform for you to shine. Showcase your talent through karaoke, live instruments, or original compositions. With fair competition, engaging rounds, and creative freedom, these categories promise an exciting and harmonious experience for all music enthusiasts.

Solo Singing

  • Participants will choose to sing a song of their choice.

  • The song should not be less than three minutes nor more than four minutes.

  • Participants can play either karaoke or bring up to three musicians to play the background music (with their own instruments).

  • Two rounds will be conducted, and participants who win the first round will proceed to the finals.

  • Any use of offensive language will result in disqualification.

Instrumentals

  • The performance should be between three to four minutes.

  • The event management will not be held liable for any loss or damage.

  • Participants are required to bring their own equipment, including instruments, wires, pedals, etc., and keep this equipment under constant inspection.

  • A backup instrumental or one separate musician for the background music is necessary.

Duo Performances

  • Participants will choose to sing a song of their choice.

  • The song should not be less than three minutes nor more than four minutes.

  • Participants can play either karaoke or bring up to three musicians to play the background music (with their own instruments).

  • Two rounds will be conducted, and participants who win the first round will proceed to the finals.

  • Any use of offensive language will result in disqualification.

Battle of the Bands

  • Participants will perform as a band with a minimum of three members.

  • The performance should include at least two non-vocalists.

  • The song should not be less than three minutes nor more than four minutes.

  • Participants can play either karaoke or bring their own instruments.

  • Two rounds will be conducted, and bands that win the first round will proceed to the finals.

  • Any use of offensive language will result in disqualification.

Old-School Hip-Hop

  • Participants will be performing solo with self-written lyrics (rap).

  • Lyrics must be submitted to the respective person three days prior to the performance for approval beforehand.

  • Beats/background tracks must be sent to the respective person three days before the performance.

  • Any use of foul language will result in immediate disqualification.

  • Diss tracks are strictly prohibited.
