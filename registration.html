REGISTRATION Details

AND DEADLINES


  • The Registration Form can be accessed through the event's official Instagram @misaal.bcpjt.

  • The Registration Form can be accessed through the event's official website.

  • Private delegates and delegations are allowed to participate in this event.

  • The registration form is open to every institution.

  • Students from ages 14 - 22 are eligible to participate.

  • Multiple delegations can be registered from a single institution; in such cases, you are requested to differentiate between delegations (e.g., Institution A & Institution B).

  • Each Delegation must have a minimum of 5 delegates and a maximum of 8 delegates.

  • All delegates must consist of a minimum participation in 1 category and a maximum of 3 categories.

  • Wait for a confirmation email after completing the registration process.

  • Payment for registration can be submitted to the campus office or online.

  • The deadline for Early Bird registration and payment is 15th January, 2025.

  • The deadline for Regular registration and payment is 30th January, 2025.

Payments must be submitted to the college office during the following times:

  • Monday to Thursday: 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM

  • Friday: 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM

For online payments, please use the following account details:

  • Account Number: 0167975001

  • Account Holder: Arfa Bushra

  • Account Type: Dubai Islamic Bank
